FNF Mobile FAQs

If you're new to this, here's everything you need to know, straight up

What is Friday Night Funkin' (FNF)?

Friday Night Funkin' is an addictive, open-source rhythm game where you battle opponents through musical duels. It's become a cultural phenomenon with its catchy music and challenging gameplay.

What is FNF Mobile?

FNF Mobile is the official mobile version of Friday Night Funkin' designed specifically for Android and iPhone devices, bringing the rhythm gaming experience to your smartphone.

What are FNF Mods?

FNF Mods are fan-made expansions that add new content, characters, songs, and gameplay mechanics to the base game, expanding the Friday Night Funkin' universe with creative community content.
